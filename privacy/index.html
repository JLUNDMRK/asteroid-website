Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Asteroid Audiobook Player is an ad-free hobby project. We respect your privacy and believe you should retain ownership of your data. The app does not sell your personal data.

1. Google Drive access and local cache

Asteroid lets you choose an audiobook folder in your own Google Drive using Google's file picker. The app indexes and reads only the Drive files and folders you explicitly select.

To make playback reliable and reduce network usage, Asteroid may download and cache audiobook files, metadata, cover images, playback progress, bookmarks, and related app data locally on your device. This local cache is used by the app for playback, offline access, and restoring app state.

Asteroid stores its local cache and app data in storage areas provided by Android for the app. The app does not scan your device for audiobook files outside the locations you choose.

Asteroid does not upload your audiobook files to our servers. We do not operate a server that receives, stores, or redistributes your audiobook library.

When you sign in with Google, the app requests limited Drive access:

  • drive.file — read and download files only inside folders you pick.
  • drive.appdata — optional backup of listening progress, bookmarks, and metadata JSON in your private app-data folder on your Drive account.

Credentials and tokens stay on your device and are sent only to Google's official APIs. We do not receive your Google credentials or file contents on our own servers.

2. Public metadata lookups

During library scan, Asteroid may query public services such as Open Library (and Google Books in some builds) using book title and author text derived from your selected Drive folders and tags. Queries are anonymous and do not include personal identifiers beyond what is required for a network request (such as your IP address).

3. Crash reporting

Anonymous crash reports may be sent via Google Firebase Crashlytics to improve stability. Reports can include device model, OS version, and stack traces. They are not used for advertising.

4. In-app contributions (tip jar)

Optional Supporter Status is handled through Google Play Billing. Payment processing is performed by Google; we do not receive your payment card details.

5. Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. It is designed to work with files and accounts you control.

6. Contact

Questions about this policy: asteroid@jlundmark.org or an issue on the GitHub repository.
