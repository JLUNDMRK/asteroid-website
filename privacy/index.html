Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Asteroid Audiobook Player is an ad-free hobby project. We respect your privacy and believe you should retain ownership of your data. Asteroid does not run any servers. We do not operate a backend that receives, stores, or redistributes your audiobook library. Your data lives only on your device and in the Google Drive areas you choose. The app does not sell your personal data.

1. Local storage and file access

To support playback, offline listening, and restoring app state, Asteroid stores cached files on your device in storage areas provided by Android for the app. This may include downloaded audiobook files, metadata, cover images, playback progress, bookmarks, and related app data.

This data stays on your device unless you choose to use optional Google Drive backup (see below). Nothing is sent to Asteroid—there is no Asteroid backend to receive it.

2. Google Drive

You sign in with Google and pick a Drive folder for your library, or use optional cloud backup in a private app-data area on your own Drive account. Asteroid may access folders you explicitly select using Google's file picker. The app indexes, reads, and plays media only from folders you choose. File contents and folder indexes are not sent to Asteroid—there are no Asteroid servers to receive them.

Technical:

  • drive.file — read and download files only inside folders you pick.
  • drive.appdata — optional backup of listening progress, bookmarks, and metadata JSON in your private app-data folder.

Credentials and tokens stay on your device and are sent only to Google's official APIs. They are never sent to an Asteroid server—Asteroid does not operate one.

3. Public metadata lookups

During library scan, Asteroid may query public services such as Open Library using book title and author text derived from your folders and tags. Queries are anonymous and do not include personal identifiers beyond what is required for a network request (such as your IP address).

4. Crash reporting

Anonymous crash reports may be sent via Google Firebase Crashlytics to improve stability. Reports can include device model, OS version, and stack traces. They are not used for advertising.

5. In-app contributions (tip jar)

Optional Supporter Status is handled through Google Play Billing. Payment processing is performed by Google; we do not receive your payment card details.

6. Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. It is designed to work with files and accounts you control.
