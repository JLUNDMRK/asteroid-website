Troubleshooting

Quick checks for common issues. Last updated: July 16, 2026

No books after I pick a folder
Confirm you chose the folder that actually contains audiobook folders (not an empty parent). Stay on a network, open Status, and wait for Index to finish. Then pull to refresh Browse.
Books appear but covers or titles look wrong
Prefer folders named Author - Title and clean ID3 tags. Open the book, edit Author/Title if needed, then resolve again. Unmatched books can appear under Browse → Unresolved.
Play waits a long time / never starts
The current track must download (or be cached). Check network and Status for download errors. Try another track, or pause other heavy downloads. Low storage can also block downloads.
Sound stops when I leave the app
Keep media playback notification permission allowed on Android 13+. Check battery optimization is not killing the player for Asteroid.
I lost my place in the book
Open Listening History (clock icon) and restore an earlier position. Cross-device progress needs cloud sync enabled and a Google account for Drive app data when you use that feature.
Cloud sign-in fails (Dropbox / OneDrive / Google sync)
Use a normal network (not a captive portal). Sign out and sign in again. For OneDrive, the Azure app registration must include the signature hash for your install type (debug vs Play).
Sync / progress does not update on another phone
Enable progress sync under Settings, stay signed in to Google for app-data sync, and wait after pause (writes are debounced). Pull Latest Played or reopen the book on the other device.
App feels stuck indexing
Indexing prefers the app in the foreground (or while playing). Check Status for stalls or sign-in issues. Pause and resume from Status, or leave the app open on Wi‑Fi for a while.

Still stuck? Mail asteroid@jlundmark.org with your app version (Settings → About) and a short description of what you tried.
